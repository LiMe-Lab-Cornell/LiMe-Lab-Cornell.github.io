Helena Aparicio

Principal Investigator

My research focuses on understanding what aspects of linguistic meaning are hard-coded as opposed to derived during linguistic interactions. To answer this question, I combines insights from theoretical linguistics, and cognitive science more broadly, with experimental and computational methods.

John R. Starr is a PhD student in the Department of Linguistics.
