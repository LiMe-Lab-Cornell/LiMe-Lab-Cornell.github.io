PARTICIPATE

Thank you for your interest in participating in our studies! The majority of our experiments are web-based. Most of our experiments are posted in crowd sourcing platforms such as Prolific or Mechanical Turk. You can find our studies by searching 'LiMe Lab @ Cornell' on either platform.

We also run in-lab experiments. If you are local to the Ithaca area and would like to participate in one of our experiments, please send us an email to We will continue to update this page with information about ongoing in-lab studies.
